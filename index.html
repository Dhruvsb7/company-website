Company Profile

At Brihaspati Non-Ferrous Metal Foundry, we specialize in manufacturing high-integrity castings in copper alloys and stainless steel. Located in the KIADB Industrial Area, Kittur, we deliver solutions for the most demanding industrial requirements.
