About Brihaspati Non-Ferrous Metal Foundry

Established in 2023, we are a technologically advanced manufacturing unit specializing in high-integrity non-ferrous and stainless steel castings. With a 10,000 sq. mt. facility in Kittur, Belagavi, we combine modern infrastructure with skilled professionals.

Our process ensures secured quality assurance, inexpensive tooling, and shorter lead times. We offer complete freedom to select alloys, reducing labor and tooling costs for our partners.
