About Brihaspati Non-Ferrous Metal Foundry

Established in 2023, Brihaspati Non Ferrous Metal Foundry is a technologically advanced manufacturing unit specializing in high-integrity non-ferrous and stainless steel castings. With a 10,000 sq. mt. facility in Kittur, Belagavi, Karnataka, we combine modern infrastructure, skilled professionals, and strict quality control.

Our process provides secured quality assurance, inexpensive tooling, and shorter lead times. It proves to be cost-effective for prototype development and allows superior design for simple to highly complicated products.
